Learn our story!

Pacific Trails resort strives to be an experience like no other. Combining all of the best parts of nature and comfort to create an unforgettable experience, no matter how long you stay. From the one of a kind ocean view, to the incredible eats or the unique lodging, we vow to make your trip a once in a lifetime experience. We want you to bring the entire family as we strive to be inclsuive and have options for all ages and likes no matter what. One of our core motivations when creating the resort was for everyone to feel like they are included, along with have specialized acitives that you will not find anywhere else, whether it be our breathtaking hikes or the immense care and detail put into each and every guest's experience, we can promise your stay will be unforgettable.

Meet the Manager!

Mike is a lover of all things nature, he started at the bottom working in customer service and has risen up to manage the entire resort. He has a passion for hiking, surfing, and camping and understands the things that can make these activities fun and enjoyable for all skill levels! He strives every single day to make sure our guests have the best stay possible and will always be found walking around the resort, go say hello!
